This zine was designed by Yamil Bautista. It’s purpose is to show those visiting New York City all the “hot spots” all from one source. As someone who travels, researching through various websites, trying to figure out where to eat, drink or landmark to visit can be cumbersome. So this zine is the solution for that.

I am currently a student at BMCC majoring in Multimedia Programming. After this spring semester, I will be transferring to a four-year university to pursue my BFA. My goal is to work as in a team as Graphic Designer creating commutative pieces.
